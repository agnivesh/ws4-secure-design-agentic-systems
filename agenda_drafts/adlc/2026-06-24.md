## ADLC Agenda — June 24, 2026

Last ADLC meeting: **2026-06-17** ([Discussion #123](https://github.com/cosai-oasis/ws4-secure-design-agentic-systems/discussions/123)). A **working/strategy session**: David LaBianca walked the SIG through the **`secure-ai-tooling` control schema** and the group locked in its drafting methodology. Decisions: **brainstorm risks in a CoSAI Google Doc** (not GitHub PRs — easier review), **risks-first then controls**, **technology-agnostic** (AGT etc. are *exemplars*, not requirements), and **transitive mapping** — risks map to controls, controls map to components, risks never directly to components (every risk must have a mitigating control). Schema is **YAML** (LLM-friendly); `docs/` holds style guides, `tables/` the human-readable summaries; controls/risks map to **personas** (collections of actions, not job roles). Phase owners now **populate AI-drafted risks per phase** into the Google Doc for review. All control work still targets **`reorg`** (now also tracked as [PR #126](https://github.com/cosai-oasis/ws4-secure-design-agentic-systems/pull/126)).
